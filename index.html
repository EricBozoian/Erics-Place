Life’s Summary

The ship has taken a beating, but no storm has yet found the strength to topple me.

📍 Hometown & Travels

🏡 Born in Manchester, NH.

📍 Lives in Nashua, NH.

🧳 Has wandered far and wide across this great land, led by duty, by work, and by an insatiable need to see what lies beyond the next horizon.

👨‍💼 Occupation(s)

💼 By day, a federal servant, navigating the grand and often perplexing machinery of government.

🏐 By night, a purveyor of sport, a keeper of the court, hosting volleyball tournaments where fierce competition and grand camaraderie intertwine.

🎯 Hobbies & Passions

🏐 The Court & The Climb

🏐 Volleyball—where power and precision meet, where battles are fought not with swords, but with skill.

🥾 Hiking—where the soul is measured in footsteps, and each summit is a triumph over the self.

💎 The Stones & The Stars

💎 A man of stone—no, not in heart, but in hand. I cut, I carve, I shape gems, turning the rough into the refined.

🔬 A tinkerer, a maker, forever chasing knowledge, whether in books, in machines, or in the steady hum of creation itself.

📚 The Written Word

📚 Philosophy, history, tales of wonder—I devour them all, for the mind, like a blade, must be sharpened lest it grow dull.

🎬 The Silver Screen

🎬 Horror, action, sci-fi—stories where the impossible is made real, and reality itself is stretched to its very limits.

⚖️ Beliefs & Ideals

🔴 Politics & Principles

🔴 Far left, some might say, though I simply stand where fairness and justice demand.

✝️ Religious, but not in the way men of power prefer—my faith is in giving, in lifting others, in love without condition.

📢 Causes Close to My Heart

💰 Economic equality—where wealth does not dictate worth.

👷 Labor rights—for a man’s toil should not leave him broken.

🌎 The environment—a world given, not owned, and never to be squandered.

⚖️ Social justice—for the chains of oppression must be shattered, one link at a time.

💻 The Modern Age & The Machine

💻 Tech-savvy, some say—a wielder of code, an architect of data, a whisperer of Excel’s arcane arts.

🖥️ HTML, Java, CSS—languages of the new world, and I have learned to speak them well.

❤️ Personal Life

💍 Love & Partnership

💍 Engaged, and soon to be wed, on the fifteenth day of March, in the year of our Lord, 2025. A new chapter, a new adventure, and a love that will not falter.

⏳ Daily Habits & Oddities

🌙 A night owl—when the world sleeps, I wake, for the dark is where thoughts run deepest.

🌊 A lover of the sea—if heaven were a place, it would have sand beneath my feet and the tide at my heels.

🐈 Feline Companions

🐈 Mimi & Lulu—small in stature, great in spirit, and masters of my heart, whether I admit it or not.
